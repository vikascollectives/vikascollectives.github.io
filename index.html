welcome to my portfolio

Hello, I'm Vikas Gautam Saxophonist, Flutist and Instructor currently based in New Delhi.

A professional practioner of saxophone and metal flute with over 30 years of experience of playing in multiple forums ranging from Stage shows, Group Bands, Musical Albums and Concerts, traveled extensively South East Asia, Middle East and Subcontinents. Most of my experience is in Freelancing Instrument playing. I also have a teaching experience for more then 20 years in both saxophone and english flute.

I play as Professional Jazz and Bollywood Instumentalist, Freelancer & Performer. My main instrument is Alto but I also play Tenor and Soprano saxophone.
