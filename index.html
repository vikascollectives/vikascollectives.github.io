Hello, I'm Vikas Gautam Saxophonist, Flutist and Instructor currently based in New Delhi.

A professional practioner of saxophone and metal flute with over 25 years of experience of playing in multiple forums ranging from Stage shows, Group Bands, Musical Albums and Concerts, traveled extensively South East Asia, Middle East and Subcontinents.
