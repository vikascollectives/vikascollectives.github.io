The Method

Play a Real Song by Lesson Ten

Most beginners quit because they spend months on scales without ever playing music they love. This method flips that: technique is taught through songs.

  • Lessons 1–3: Posture, breathing, embouchure and your first clean notes.
  • Lessons 4–6: Scales and phrasing built into simple melodies.
  • Lessons 7–10: Your first complete Bollywood instrumental, performed end to end.
  • Beyond: Jazz improvisation, classical pieces, tone development and stagecraft.

Common Questions

Lesson FAQs

How long does it take to learn saxophone?

With this structured method, most students play their first complete Bollywood instrumental within ten lessons. Real fluency grows with regular practice over the following months.

Do I need my own saxophone to start?

Yes — you'll need an instrument to practice on. You'll get guidance on choosing the right beginner alto saxophone or flute within your budget before you buy anything.

Do you teach children and adults?

Yes. Lessons are tailored for school students, working professionals and retirees, from complete beginners to intermediate players.

Are online saxophone lessons effective?

Yes. Live one-on-one video lessons with structured practice material work very well for saxophone and flute; students join from across India and abroad.
