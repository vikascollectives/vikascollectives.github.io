Formats

Solo, Duo or Full Band

  • Solo saxophone / flute - elegant, space-efficient, ideal for cocktail hours and intimate gatherings.
  • Sax + backing tracks - a full, rich sound with a small footprint and simple technical needs.
  • Fusion / Indo-Jazz ensemble - a complete band arrangement for large stages and dance floors.

Repertoire spans jazz standards, Bollywood classics and current hits, Hindustani-classical-inspired pieces, and original fusion compositions on alto, tenor and soprano saxophone and English flute.

Common Questions

Booking FAQs

How much does it cost to book a saxophonist in Delhi?

It depends on the date, venue, duration and format (solo or band). Share your event details by phone, WhatsApp or email and you'll receive a same-day quote.

What kind of music do you play at events?

Jazz standards, Bollywood instrumentals, classical melodies and fusion. Every set list is curated to the occasion - romantic melodies for weddings, smooth jazz for cocktail hours, upbeat Bollywood for celebrations.

Do you perform outside Delhi NCR?

Yes - over 3,300 shows performed across India and worldwide, including Australia, London (UK), Canada, Dubai (UAE), Hong Kong, Nepal, Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Africa, the Maldives, Turkey and Bahrain. Travel for destination weddings and corporate events is welcome.

Can you perform with a full band?

Yes. Fusion, Indo-Jazz and jazz ensemble arrangements are available, built on years of residencies at leading five-star hotels.
